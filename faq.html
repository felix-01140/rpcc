General Info.

Service Times, Our main worship service begins at 10:30 AM every Sunday. We recommend arriving a few minutes early to find a seat and settle in..

We also have a sunday english service which start at 7:30AM to 9:30AM.

Revival Place Community church is located at Kg 706ST Gisozi, Gasabo. Directions and a map can be found on our Contact or Visit Us page..

Yes, we offer free parking on-site. There are also designated spots for visitors, families with young children, and those with disabilities.

About us

We believe in the authority of the Bible, the saving grace of Jesus Christ, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our core beliefs are based on historic Christian faith and can be found on our "What We Believe" page.
