About Revival Palace

Our Story, Vision, and Mission

Our History

Revival Palace Community Church was established in 2010 in Kigali, Rwanda, with a vision to create a vibrant community of believers dedicated to transforming lives through the power of God's love. What began as a small gathering of 20 people has grown into a thriving church community serving thousands of believers across Kigali.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Kigali, raising disciples who impact their communities and nation through the love of Christ. We envision a church where every member is empowered to live out their God-given purpose and contribute to the building of God's kingdom.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples, and serve our community through:

  • Dynamic worship and prayer
  • Biblical teaching and discipleship
  • Community outreach and social impact
  • Youth and family ministry
  • Leadership development
