About Revival Palace

Our Story, Vision, and Mission

Our History

Revival Palace Community Church was established in 2010 in Kigali, Rwanda, with a vision to create a vibrant community of believers dedicated to transforming lives through the power of God's love. What began as a small gathering of 20 people has grown into a thriving church community serving thousands of believers across Kigali.

Our Vision

A community-transforming church, glorifying Christ.

Community-Transforming

Catalyzing positive change in our society

Christ-Centered

Keeping Jesus at the heart of all we do

Living to honor and glorify Christ

Our Mission

We passionately spread the Gospel through evangelism, establish vibrant communities through church planting, nurture believers through discipleship, serve with compassion through charity, and enrich lives through education.

Our Values
