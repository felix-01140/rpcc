Our Ministries

Discover Your Place to Serve and Grow

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry, led by dedicated teachers and volunteers, provides a safe and nurturing environment for children to learn about God's love. We offer age-appropriate Bible lessons, worship, and activities for children from ages 3 to 12.

  • Sunday School (Ages 3-12)
  • Vacation Bible School
  • Children's Choir
  • Special Events and Activities
  • Get Involved

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry at Revival Palace is committed to empowering young people to grow in their faith and develop leadership skills. We provide a supportive community where youth can connect, learn, and serve together.

  • Youth Bible Study (Ages 13-18)
  • Youth Worship Team
  • Leadership Development
  • Community Service Projects
  • Youth Conferences and Retreats
  • Join Youth Ministry

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry creates opportunities for women to connect, grow spiritually, and support one another in their faith journey. Through various programs and events, we encourage women to discover their God-given purpose.

  • Women's Bible Study
  • Prayer Groups
  • Mentorship Program
  • Women's Conferences
  • Community Outreach
  • Join Women's Ministry

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry focuses on building strong, godly men who lead their families and communities with integrity. We provide opportunities for fellowship, discipleship, and service.

  • Men's Bible Study
  • Leadership Training
  • Accountability Groups
  • Community Service
  • Men's Retreats
  • Join Men's Ministry

Music & Worship

Our Music and Worship Ministry leads the congregation in worship through music, creating an atmosphere where people can connect with God. We welcome musicians, singers, and technical team members.

  • Adult Choir
  • Youth Worship Team
  • Instrumental Ministry
  • Technical Team
  • Worship Training
  • Join Worship Team

Community Outreach

Our Community Outreach Ministry serves the needs of our local community through various programs and initiatives, demonstrating God's love in practical ways.

  • Food Bank
  • Education Support
  • Health Programs
  • Skills Training
  • Emergency Relief
  • Volunteer

Get Involved
