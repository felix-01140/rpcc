Mission and Core Values

A community-transforming church, glorifying Christ. We passionately spread the Gospel through evangelism.

Establish vibrant communities through church planting, nurture believers through discipleship, serve with compassion through charity, and enrich lives through education.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Bible as the inspired and infallible Word of God. We believe in the Trinity, the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, His bodily resurrection, His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and His personal return in power and glory.
